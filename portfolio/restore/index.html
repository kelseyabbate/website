MississippiRiverDelta.org

Restore the Mississippi River Delta is a coalition of several regional and national environmental groups dedicated to protecting Louisiana from the continuing threat of coastal land loss.

Their website is a primary tool for promoting awareness and educating the public, but originally lacked visual appeal and organization. In 2016, I redesigned the site from the ground up by introducing strong typography, a bold color palette, and a progressive look that flowed throughout the entire site.

2017 American Web Design Award

The client was so thrilled with the finished product that I continued to work with them to design several print materials including a 40-page report, report summary, and a full page advertisment.
