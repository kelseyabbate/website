TheSaxonyCondos.com

The Saxony is a luxury condominium located in the heart of the Bywater neighborhood in New Orleans. While the building is new and modern, its locale is rooted in history. The Saxony's previous website lacked luster and appeal. They needed a site that could attract buyers and showcase its upscale amenities and offerings.

The concept for the website is a sleek modern design with subtle effects and graceful interactions. The homepage features 5 sections with controlled scrolling in order to closely engage with each part of the story.
