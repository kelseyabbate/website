StanleyReuter.com

Stanley Reuter is an elite law firm specializing in complex litigation. They pride themselves on their work and recruitment of top law school graduates. They needed a new website that reflected their expertise and practice.

My vision for the website design was to be bold & impressive, and to stand out from typical law firm websites. Instead of traditional blue, I went with a striking color palette of red, black, and dark gray tones. I paired serif and geometric sans-serif typography to create a sophisticated & modern feel.
