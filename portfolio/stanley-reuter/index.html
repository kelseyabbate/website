StanleyReuter.com

Stanley, Reuter, Ross, Thornton & Alford, LLC - abbreviated as Stanley Reuter - is an elite law firm specializing in complex litigation. They pride themselves on their work and recruiting only the top law school graduates.

My vision for the website was to design something bold and impressive that stood out from your typical law firm website. Instead of a traditional blue, I went with a striking color palette of red, black, and dark gray tones. I paired serif and geometric sans-serif type to create a sophisticated yet modern feel.
