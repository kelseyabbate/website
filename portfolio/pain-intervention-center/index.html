PainInterventionCenter.com

The Pain Intervention Center provides minimally invasive procedures for spine, neck, and back pain. Dr. Waring is an expert in the field of interventional pain management without the use of opioids. He has helped hundereds of patients find relief and manage their pain with his treatments in a safe and comfortable atmosphere.

In 2015, I developed a revamped brand for the Pain Intervention Center including a new logo and website. The logo represents the sophistication and precision of Dr. Waring's practice and expertise, along with subtle abstraction of a spine. Throughout the years I designed marketing materials such as brochures, billboards, and advertising.
