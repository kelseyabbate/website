PainInterventionCenter.com

The Pain Intervention Center provides minimally invasive procedures for spine, neck, and back pain. Dr. Waring is an expert in the field of interventional pain management without the use of opioids. His treatments have helped hundreds of patients find relief and manage their pain, and he and his staff take pride in providing a safe and comfortable atmosphere for the procedures.

In 2015, I developed a revamped brand for the Pain Intervention Center including a new logo and website. The logo represents the sophistication and precision of Dr. Waring's practice and expertise, along with subtle abstraction of a spine. I also designed marketing materials such as brochures, billboards, and advertising.

In 2019, we ran a digital campaign. The creative was split into two focuses: Dr. Waring himself, and a figurative representation of back pain. Both versions had similar messaging but fine-tuned to accompany the imagery. The doctor creative had messaging about himself and his treatments. The x-ray creative was about managing chronic pain. Both creative included a CTA to call the office.
