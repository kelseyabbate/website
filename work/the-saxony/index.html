The Saxony Condos

A stylish website for a luxury condominium in a hip neighborhood of New Orleans.

The Saxony is a luxury condominium located in the funky Bywater neighborhood of New Orleans. While the building is new, its location is rooted in culture. The Saxony’s previous website lacked luster and appeal. They needed a site that could attract buyers and showcase its upscale amenities.

Client
Ward Investments
Agency
Deep Fried Advertising
Active
2018
Role
Lead Designer
Work
  • UX Strategy
  • Website Design

The concept for the website is a sleek, modern design with subtle effects and graceful interactions. A fixed vertical header nods to the building’s architectural design. The homepage features colorful sections with controlled scrolling in order to closely engage with each part of the story.
