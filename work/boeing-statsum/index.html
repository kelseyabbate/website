STATSUM Report

Editorial design for the annual Statistical Summary of Commercial Jet Airplane Accidents.

STATSUM is a commercial aviation accident data report that has been published annually since 1959. I designed the layout and data visualization for the 2022 and 2023 editions. It's produced and distributed as a digital PDF and features an interactive vertical navigation.
