Operation Spark

A website design concept for a digital education organization.

Operation Spark is a nonprofit that offers coding & software development immersion programs to low-opportunity teens and adults. In 2021, I designed a new concept for their website. Their existing site lacked appeal and excitement. My vision was to design an energizing, electric, youthful, and attractive layout with bright colors, bold typography, and playful visual elements that coalesce with the OpSpark brand.
