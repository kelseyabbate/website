Maritime Partners

A stylish website for a luxury condominium in a hip New Orleans neighborhood.

The Saxony is a luxury condominium located in the funky Bywater neighborhood of New Orleans. While the building is new, its locale is rooted in culture. The Saxony's previous website lacked luster and appeal. They needed a site that could attract buyers and showcase its upscale amenities.

Client
Maritime Partners, LLC
Agency
Deep Fried Advertising
Active
2020-present
Role
Lead Design
Work
  • UX Strategy
  • Website Design

The concept for the website is a sleek, modern design with subtle effects and graceful interactions. A fixed vertical header nods to the building's architectural design. The homepage features five sections with controlled scrolling in order to closely engage with each part of the story.
