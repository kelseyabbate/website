Maritime Partners

A state-of-the-art website for a modern maritime financing company.

Maritime Partners offers leasing and financing services to the Jones Act trade and beyond with their impressive fleet of vessels. They have decades of experience in asset management, financing, and shipbuilding. They are experts in their field and routinely publish insights on the latest market data. In order to attract investors and showcase themselves as leaders of the industry, I designed a new website with a clean, sharp, modern look.
