Triton Stone Group

Product catalogs, print design, and web design for a regional stone and tile company.

Triton Stone is the largest natural stone importer in the Southeastern United States with an extensive inventory of stone slabs, tile, and kitchen & bath fixtures. In 2017, I designed several marketing & collateral materials along with a complete redesign of their website.

Printed works include a stone & tile catalog, sinks & faucets catalog, print ads, and postcards & business cards for their 20+ locations. I also created a social graphic and email blast template.

The clean and polished-looking website showcases the company's high-end stone, tile, and plumbing products, along with their growing locations and insights on home design trends.
