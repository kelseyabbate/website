Community Guide to Coastal Restoration

A booklet and website integration for a quick reference guide to Louisiana coastal restoration information.

I designed and formatted a pocket-sized “Community Guide to Coastal Restoration.” It features 15 chapters of information on organizations, acts of legislation and policies, and other coastal restoration references.

Client

Agency

Work

To accompany the handbook, I designed a web component that was integrated into their website. It serves as a digital/interactive version of the printed book where users can read through the chapters. The webpages mirror the handbook along with links and contact information that can be updated routinely.
