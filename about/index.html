I'm an award-winning Senior Designer & UX Strategist in New Orleans, Louisiana with 9+ years of experience in a fast-paced creative and interactive agency.

My background in graphic design and web development allows me to work on multidisiciplinary projects including creative strategy, branding, websites, and print/digital design.

As a Senior Designer I strive to create beautiful and simplistic solutions that effectively get the message across. I've created logos, posters, brochures, signage, reports & books, and a variety of large and small custom websites. I primarily focus on advertising, layouts, website design, and brand decks.

As a UX Strategist, I use my knowledge of web development to inform how I design a website to make it not only beautiful but functional and easy for the user. I work with creative stategists to develop sitemaps and ensure a cohesive, branded treatment across print and digital media. I have the unique position of being a design liason, bridging the gap between Creative and DevOps teams.

Additionally, I support Sales, Accounts, and Operations teams with project scopes, decks & processes.
