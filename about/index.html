I’m an award-winning Senior Visual & UX/UI Designer with 10+ years of experience in fast-paced, creative and interactive agencies.

With a background in Graphic Design and experience in UX Strategy and Front-End Web Development, I collaborate with copywriters, engineers, strategists, and managers on multidisciplinary projects including: creative strategy, branding, websites, print design, and digital design.

As a Visual Designer I strive to create beautiful and simplistic solutions that effectively get the message across. I’ve created logos, posters, brochures, signage, reports & books, and a variety of large and small custom websites. I primarily focus on advertising, layouts, website design, and brand decks. I’m keen on attention to detail, diligence, and efficiency.

As a UX Designer/Strategist, I use my knowledge of web development to inform how I design a website to make it not only beautiful but functional, engaging, and intuitive. I collaborate with stategists and developers to create sitemaps and structures that ensure a cohesive, branded treatment across print and digital media. I have held the unique position to act as a design liaison, bridging the gap between Creative and DevOps.

Additionally, I support sales, client services, and operations teams with contributions to project scopes, decks, and internal processes.
